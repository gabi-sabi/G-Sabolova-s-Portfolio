The Best Movies According to Gabi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Anna Karenina

The newest adaptation of this classic with Keira Knightley has it all - romance, love, drama!

Sherlock Holmes

If you enjoy suspense, witty dialogue and epic adventures, this one is deffinitely for you. Enojy!

Inside Out

Great fun for the whole family. Comedy about what actually happens inside our brains when we are happy, angry or in love.
